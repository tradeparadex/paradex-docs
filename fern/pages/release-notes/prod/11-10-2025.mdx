## v1.120.0

<ChangelogTags tags={["UI", "API"]} />

* `TPSL` Modal can now be dragged and repositioned on screen for better visibility.
* Fixed issue where all `TWAP` orders were canceled if any sub-order was canceled due to `exceeded slippage`.
* Disabled maximum slippage check for `LIMIT` orders.
* Fixed race condition when modifying `TP/SL` orders that could cause the account to become stuck and prevent new order creation.
