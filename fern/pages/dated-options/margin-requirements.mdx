---
title: Margin requirements
---

Paradex supports two margin modes for Dated Options: **Cross Margin** for all users and **[Portfolio Margin](/trading/portfolio-margin)** (currently in beta for whitelisted users).

## Cross margin

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buying options">
    $$
    \text{Margin} = \min(\text{Option Mark Price},~\text{Long ITM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price})
    $$
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Selling options">
    $$
    \text{Margin} = \max(\text{Short ITM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price} - \text{OTM Amount},~\text{Short OTM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price})
    $$

    where OTM Amount is:
    - **Calls:** max(0, Strike Price - Spot Price)
    - **Puts:** max(0, Spot Price - Strike Price)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Cross margin parameters

| Parameter | IMR | MMR |
|-----------|:---:|:---:|
| Premium Multiplier | 100% | 50% |
| Long ITM Fraction | 20% | 10% |
| Short ITM Fraction | 15% | 7.5% |
| Short OTM Fraction | 10% | 5% |
| Short Put Cap | 50% | 25% |
