title: Fees
---

Dated Option fees are expressed as a percentage of the underlying notional. All trading fees are capped at **12.5% of the option premium**.

## Orderbook fees

| Profile | Maker | Taker |
|---------|:-----:|:-----:|
| Retail | 0% | 0% |
| Pro | 0.01% | vs Pro: 0.02%, vs Retail: 0.005% |

## Paradigm RFQ

| | Fee |
|--|:---:|
| Maker | 0.01% |
| Taker | 0% |

## Settlement fee

A **0.01%** fee is applied at settlement.
