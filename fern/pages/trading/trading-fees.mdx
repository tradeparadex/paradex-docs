Trading fees vary based on your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles).

<Info>
All fees are calculated based on notional value. Dated option fees are capped at 12.5% of the option premium.
</Info>

## Settlement Fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|                          | Fee    |
|--------------------------|:------:|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |
