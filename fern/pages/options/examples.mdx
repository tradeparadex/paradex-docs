    ## üìà **Long Call (Bullish Bet)**

    ### üîç Setup

    - **BTC Spot Price**: $85,381.9
    - **Strategy**: Alice is bullish and buys a Call Option ‚Äî **BTC-USD-88000-C**
    - **Strike Price**: $88,000
    - **Ask Price**: $248.9
    - **Mark Price**: $192.9

    ### üßæ Order Entry

    - Alice can choose to enter the order size in either BTC or USD Spot Notional (Size x Spot Price) terms.
    - Alice enters a **\$2,000 USD Notional** ‚Üí `0.023 BTC` or **(2,000 / 85,381)**
    - She submits a **market buy** at $248.9

    - **Position Opened**:
      - Size: **0.023 BTC**
      - Entry Price: **\$248.9**
      - Spot Notional = Size x Spot Price= `0.023 √ó 85,381` ‚âà **\$1,963**
      - **8h Funding Rate**: 0.0749% ‚Üí Expected funding cost per 8h = `0.023 √ó 0.000749 √ó 85,381` ‚âà **\$1.47**
      - **Unrealized PnL at Entry** = `0.023 √ó (192.9 - 248.9)` ‚âà **1.30 USD**
        - Alice has a slight Unrealized PnL at Entry because the Mark Price **(\$192.9)** is below the Entry Price **(\$248.9\)**

    ### ‚è±Ô∏è After 12 Hours

    - **Spot Price** rises from **\$85,381** to **\$87,000**
    - **Mark Price** rises from **\$192.9** to **\$503**
    - **Avg 8h Funding Rate (over 12 hours)**: 0.08%
    - **Avg Spot Price (over 12 hours)**: \$86,000
    - **Funding Cost** ‚âà `0.023 √ó 0.0008 √ó 12 √∑ 8 √ó 86,000` ‚âà **\$2.37**

    ### üí∞ Final PnL

    - Option PnL: `0.023 √ó (503 - 248.9)` ‚âà **+5.84 USD**
    - Less Funding: **2.37 USD**
    - **Total Unrealized PnL**: **+3.47 USD**
    - **PnL %**: `3.47 √∑ (0.023 √ó 248.9)` ‚âà <span style="color: green; font-weight: bold;">+61%</span>
