# Builder Codes

Builder Codes let developers and AI agent creators earn revenue by integrating Paradex into their tools, bots, or signals.

## Integration

To integrate a Builder Code, include your code as a parameter when submitting orders through the Paradex API or MCP tools. This links trading activity to your integration and enables revenue tracking.

```json
{
  "market_id": "BTC-USD-PERP",
  "order_side": "BUY",
  "order_type": "MARKET",
  "size": 0.01,
  "builder_code": "your_builder_code"
}
```

## Revenue Structure

Builders earn a share of fees generated by orders submitted with their Builder Code. Revenue is calculated based on trading volume attributed to your integration and distributed on a regular basis.

Contact the Paradex team via [Discord](https://discord.gg/paradex) to apply for a Builder Code.
