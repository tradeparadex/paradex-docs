# Quickstart guide

This guide helps you connect the Paradex MCP server to your AI assistant in minutes.

Your setup choice depends on two things:

- **Which MCP features you need** — trading is only available with a local setup at the moment. Remote and Paradex-hosted options provide market data only.
- **Which AI client you prefer** — Claude Desktop, Cursor, ChatGPT, and others are supported. Not every client supports every setup path.

<Steps>

### Pick your setup path

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Local MCP setup (recommended)">

MCP server runs on your machine. Full API support including trading.

1. Complete the [Prerequisites](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp/prerequisites) — install Python, uvx, and get your Paradex private key.
2. Go to [Connect your client](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp/connect-your-client) and follow the instructions for your AI client.

</Tab>
<Tab title="Remote MCP deployment">

You host your own MCP server on a cloud platform (Railway, Render, Fly.io, etc.) and connect your AI client to it via a URL. Market data only — no trading support at the moment.

1. Follow the [Remote Deployment guide](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp/remote-deployment) to deploy your server.
2. Go to [Connect your client](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp/connect-your-client) and enter your endpoint URL.

</Tab>
<Tab title="Paradex-hosted MCP">

The fastest way to get started — no installation, no cloud deployment. Market data only — no trading support at the moment.

Follow the [Paradex-hosted MCP guide](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp/connect-to-paradex-mcp) to connect via URL or Smithery.

</Tab>
</Tabs>
