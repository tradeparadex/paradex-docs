---
title: Margin requirements
---

Paradex supports two margin modes for Dated Options: **Cross Margin** for all users and **[Portfolio Margin](/risk/portfolio-margin)** (currently in beta for whitelisted users).

## Cross margin

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buying options">
    $$
    \text{Margin} = \min\big(\text{Option Mark Price},
    ~\text{Long ITM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price}\big)
    $$
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Selling options">
    $$
    \begin{align*}
    \text{Margin} = \max\big(
    &\text{Short ITM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price} - \text{OTM Amount}, \\
    &\text{Short OTM Fraction} \times \text{Spot Price}\big)
    \end{align*}
    $$

    where OTM Amount is:
    - **Calls:** max(0, Strike Price - Spot Price)
    - **Puts:** max(0, Spot Price - Strike Price)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
