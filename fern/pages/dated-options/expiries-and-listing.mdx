## Available expiries

Paradex lists Dated Options across three expiry cycles:

| Cycle | Contracts listed | Expiry day |
|-------|-----------------|------------|
| Daily | 1, 2, 3 days out | Daily at 08:00 UTC |
| Weekly | 1, 2, 3 weeks out | Friday at 08:00 UTC |
| Monthly | 1, 2, 3 months out | Last Friday of the month at 08:00 UTC |

<Note>
**CL (Crude Oil):** Only 2 weekly expiries are listed (1w and 2w). Daily and monthly cycles are not available for CL. If an expiry falls on a weekend or public holiday (per the Pyth CL calendar), it rolls to the next business day.
</Note>

## Listing schedule

New contracts are listed automatically on the following schedule:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily">
    Listed every day at **04:00 UTC**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Weekly">
    Listed every **Thursday at 04:00 UTC**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Monthly">
    Listed on the **last Thursday of each month** at 04:00 UTC.

    These options expire on the last Friday of the following month.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Strike listing

Strikes are dynamically classified based on call delta:

| Moneyness | Call delta range |
|-----------|----------------|
| ATM | 0.40 - 0.60 |
| Outer | 0.20 - 0.40 and 0.60 - 0.80 |
| Wings | < 0.20 or > 0.80 |

Strikes are automatically listed for deltas between **0.10 and 0.90**, and the listing process runs every **8 hours** at 04:00, 12:00, and 20:00 UTC.
