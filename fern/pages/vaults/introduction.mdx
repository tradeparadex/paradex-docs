## What are VTFs?

Vault Traded Funds (VTFs) are smart contract based asset pools where investors
deposit collateral and receive VTF Tokens representing their share. These pooled
assets are managed by Operators who employ trading strategies to generate returns
for depositors.

VTFs on Paradex represent the next generation of on chain investment management,
building on what previous vault protocols started and taking it further with
multi asset support, portfolio margin, and LP token composability.

## Who should use VTFs?

- An Investor looking for higher yields or returns? → Become a Depositor
- An Asset Manager looking for a larger capital base? → Become an Operator

## Benefits for Investors (Depositors)

1. **Access to Professional Trading Strategies**\
   Benefit from experienced operators who employ sophisticated strategies across
   various markets including perpetual futures, options, and spot.
2. **Potential for Enhanced Returns**\
   Pooling your assets with others allows participation in more scalable trading
   strategies not typically accessible to individual investors.
3. **Greater Diversification**\
   Invest across multiple VTFs of different risk profiles and strategy types to
   achieve diversification benefits.
4. **Transparent and Secure Management**\
   VTF operations are governed by smart contracts on the blockchain, providing
   full transparency and allowing you to monitor your assets at any time.

## Benefits for Asset Managers (Operators)

1. **Access to Larger Capital Pools**\
   Manage significant pooled assets from multiple depositors, enabling you to
   execute larger trades and enhance profit potential.
2. **Configurable Parameters**\
   Set profit sharing percentages between 0% and 30% to attract investors and
   reward your expertise. Operators can also configure other parameters like the
   lockup period and the max TVL.
3. **Order Privacy**\
   The off chain order book ensures your orders are fully protected from front
   running, ensuring trades are executed without external manipulation.
4. **Operational Efficiency**\
   Streamlined VTF management with efficient withdrawals and automated processes
   reduces administrative overhead.
5. **Reputation Building**\
   Successfully managing a VTF and delivering consistent returns builds
   credibility, attracting more depositors and opening doors within the DeFi
   ecosystem.
