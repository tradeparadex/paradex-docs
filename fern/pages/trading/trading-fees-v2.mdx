<Note title="Transition period" icon="fa-solid fa-circle-info">
The following volume-based fee tiers and discounts come into effect on April 17, 1PM SGT with a transition period of one week until April 22, 1PM SGT. During the transition period, all users will enjoy VIP 6 and Pro 4 tier fees for Retail and Pro respectively. Additionally, during the transition period, all users will receive Tier 7 (Obsidian) staking discounts of 30%. These measures ensure all users have sufficient time to stake \$DIME and consolidate trading volume. The \$DIME fee discount (if users opt in to pay fees in \$DIME) is effective immediately on April 17, 1PM SGT.
</Note>

## Staking discounts (Pro & Retail)

Users who stake \$DIME receive a discount on their Spot & Perps trading fees. The discount scales with the amount staked, ranging from 0% up to 30%.

- Applies to the user's volume-based taker fees
- Stackable with \$DIME fee discount

| Staking Discount Tier | Staking Amount (\$DIME) | Fee Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | — | 0% |
| Tier 2 | 10K | 5% |
| Tier 3 | 50K | 10% |
| Tier 4 | 100K | 15% |
| Tier 5 | 500K | 20% |
| Tier 6 | 1M | 25% |
| Tier 7 | 2M | 30% |

## \$DIME fee discounts (Pro & Retail)

Users who opt to pay their Spot & Perps trading fees in \$DIME receive an additional flat discount. \$DIME fees are deducted from the user's spot \$DIME deposit on Paradex.

- Flat 20% discount, stackable on top of staking discount
- Mark price exchange rate (\$DIME ↔ USDC) at the time of the user's transaction applies

<Tip title="Example" icon="fa-solid fa-lightbulb">
If a user stakes 100K \$DIME (15% staking discount) and pays trading fees in \$DIME (20% \$DIME fee discount), the ultimate discount rate would be 35% (15% + 20%) applied to the user's volume-based taker fee.
</Tip>

## Trading volume tiers (Retail) — Spot & Perps

Retail taker fees for Spot & Perps are determined by the user's 14-day (14D) Spot & Perps trading volume across main account and all subaccounts.

Holders of a [MoneyBadgers](https://opensea.io/collection/moneybadgers) or [ShizoPunks](https://opensea.io/collection/shizopunks) NFT on their connected wallet qualify for reduced volume thresholds as shown below.

| Tier | 14D Volume (USD) | OR | 14D Volume (USD) + NFT | Taker Fee | After Max DC (50%) | Maker Fee |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Regular (VIP 0) | — | OR | — | 0.0024% | 0.0012% | 0% |
| VIP 1 | ≥ 100K | OR | NFT | 0.002% | 0.001% | 0% |
| VIP 2 | ≥ 500K | OR | ≥ 250K + NFT | 0.0018% | 0.0009% | 0% |
| VIP 3 | ≥ 1M | OR | ≥ 500K + NFT | 0.0016% | 0.0008% | 0% |
| VIP 4 | ≥ 5M | OR | ≥ 2.5M + NFT | 0.0014% | 0.0007% | 0% |
| VIP 5 | ≥ 10M | OR | ≥ 6M + NFT | 0.0012% | 0.0006% | 0% |
| VIP 6 | ≥ 50M | OR | ≥ 35M + NFT | 0.001% | 0.0005% | 0% |

<sub>Fees are calculated on trade notional (Size × Trade Price).</sub>

## Trading volume tiers (Pro) — Spot & Perps

Pro taker fees for Spot & Perps are determined by the user's 14-day (14D) Spot & Perps trading volume across main account and all subaccounts.

| Tier | 14D Volume (USD) | Taker Fee | After Max DC (50%) | Maker Fee |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pro 0 | — | 0.045% | 0.0225% | 0% |
| Pro 1 | ≥ 1M | 0.042% | 0.021% | 0% |
| Pro 2 | ≥ 5M | 0.039% | 0.0195% | 0% |
| Pro 3 | ≥ 10M | 0.037% | 0.0185% | 0% |
| Pro 4 | ≥ 25M | 0.035% | 0.0175% | 0% |

### FastFills discount

Pro taker fees are reduced by an additional 30% (stackable with staking and \$DIME fee discounts) when matching against retail orders, with minimum fees capped at 1 bps (0.01%). See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

<sub>Fees are calculated on trade notional (Size × Trade Price).</sub>

## Options fees (Pro & Retail)

Fees are capped at 12.5% of the option price (premium). FastFills discount applies to the fee amount.

| Profile | Maker Fee | Taker Fee |
|---|---|---|
| <Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0075% |
| <Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0125% |

### FastFills discount

Options Pro taker fees are reduced by an additional 30% when matching against retail orders. See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

## Settlement fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

| Market | Fee |
|---|---|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |
