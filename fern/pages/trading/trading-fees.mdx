## How fees work

Your Spot & Perps trading fee is the **base rate** set by your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles) and 14-day (14D) trading volume, minus any discounts that apply. Fees are charged on trade notional (Size × Trade Price).

`Your fee = base rate × (1 − total discount)`

Maker fees are 0% for both profiles. Pro fees are subject to a minimum of 1.75 bps (0.0175%), regardless of total discount.

## Discounts

Three discounts are additive and apply to your volume-based taker fee:

- [**Stake \$DIME**](#stake-dime): up to 30% off, scaling with the amount staked.
- [**Pay fees in \$DIME**](#pay-fees-in-dime): flat 40% off.
- [**FastFills**](#fastfills-pro-only): 30% off for Pro orders matching Retail.

### Stake \$DIME

Stake \$DIME to unlock a tiered discount on your Spot & Perps taker fees, scaling up to 30%. See [How does staking work?](#faq).

| Staking Tier | Staking Amount (\$DIME) | Fee Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Wood | 10K | 5% |
| Bronze | 50K | 10% |
| Silver | 100K | 15% |
| Gold | 500K | 20% |
| Platinum | 1M | 25% |
| Diamond | 2M | 30% |

### Pay fees in \$DIME

Pay your Spot & Perps trading fees in \$DIME for a **flat 40% discount**, deducted from your spot \$DIME deposit on Paradex. The mark price exchange rate (\$DIME ↔ USDC) at the time of the user's transaction applies.

<Tip title="Example" icon="fa-solid fa-lightbulb">
A user who stakes 100K \$DIME (15% staking discount) and pays fees in \$DIME (40% \$DIME fee discount) gets a total discount of 55% (15% + 40%), applied to their volume-based taker fee.
</Tip>

### FastFills (Pro only)

Pro taker fees are reduced by an additional **30%** when matching against Retail orders, stackable with staking and \$DIME fee discounts. The 1.75 bps Pro minimum still applies. See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

## Other fees

### Options

Fees are capped at 12.5% of the option price (premium). The FastFills 30% discount applies to Pro taker fees matching Retail orders.

| Profile | Maker Fee | Taker Fee |
|---|---|---|
| <Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0075% |
| <Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0125% |

### Settlement

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee applies to any positions still open.

| Market | Fee |
|---|---|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |

Stake \$DIME to unlock tiered fee discounts ranging from 5% (Wood) to 30% (Diamond), applied to your volume-based taker fees and stackable with the \$DIME fee discount.

To unstake, submit an unstaking request at any time. A 24-hour cooldown follows, after which you must confirm to receive your funds.
