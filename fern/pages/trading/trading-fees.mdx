## Trading Profiles for Futures
1. UI (Retail) traders enjoy completely <Badge intent="success" minimal>FREE</Badge> trading
2. API traders have access to <Badge intent="note" minimal>Standard</Badge> rates

## Fee Structure

Fees are calculated based on notional value.

| | Perpetual Futures  | Perpetual Options |
|---|:---|:---|
| <Tooltip tip="UI Default"><Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge></Tooltip> |  <Tooltip tip="Powered by RPI"><Badge intent="success" minimal>FREE (0%)</Badge></Tooltip>  | Maker: -0.01% <br /> Taker : 0.03% |
| <Tooltip tip="API Default"><Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Standard</Badge></Tooltip> | Maker: 0.002% <br /> <Tooltip tip={<div><Icon icon="fa-regular fa-bolt" /> <strong>FastFills Taker Discount</strong> <Icon icon="fa-regular fa-bolt" /><br />Standard taker fee is reduced by 75% to 0.005% when matching against retail orders.</div>} >Taker (vs retail): 0.005% <Icon icon="fa-regular fa-bolt" /></Tooltip> <br /> Taker (vs standard): 0.02%   | Maker: -0.01% <br /> Taker : 0.03% |

## Settlement Fees

When a market is delisted, Paradex charges a fee to close any positions still open in that market.

|         | Fee    | Cap |
|---------|:------:|:---:|
| Futures | 0.015% | N/A |
| Options | 0.010% | 25% |
