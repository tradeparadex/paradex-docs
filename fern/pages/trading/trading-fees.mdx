## Dated Options

Dated Option fees are expressed as a percentage of the underlying notional. All trading fees are capped at **12.5% of the option premium**.

## Settlement Fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|                          | Fee    |
|--------------------------|:------:|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.01% |

<sub>Fees are calculated based on notional value.</sub>
