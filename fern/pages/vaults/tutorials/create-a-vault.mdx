<Steps>

    Enter the details to create your VTF.
    <img src="../../../assets/vaults/vault_create_form.png" alt=""/>
  ### Initial Deposit
    Follow the steps in [deposit](vault-deposit "mention") to activate your VTF with a first deposit.
</Steps>
