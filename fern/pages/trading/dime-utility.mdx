\$DIME is the native token of the Paradex ecosystem. It powers fee discounts, protocol buybacks, and will serve as the gas token for Paradex Chain.

### Pay Fees in \$DIME

Pay fees in \$DIME for a flat 40% discount. Fees are deducted from your spot \$DIME balance at the \$DIME ↔ USDC mark price at transaction time.

<Tip title="Example" icon="fa-solid fa-lightbulb">
A user who stakes 100K \$DIME (15% staking discount) and pays fees in \$DIME (40% \$DIME fee discount) gets a total discount of 55% (15% + 40%), applied to their volume based taker fee.
</Tip>

For the full fee schedule and volume tiers, see [Trading Fees](/trading/trading-fees).

## Buybacks

Net revenue generated from protocol fees will be used to buy back \$DIME and/or fund ecosystem growth initiatives. Buybacks are executed periodically (e.g. Daily TWAP) using available net revenue.

The account that conducts buybacks is `0x3f38009a58bfc930a399ef9670ee60b355d9d22ea79709f2243182989d16709` and its \$DIME balance can be viewed [here](https://app.paradex.trade/dime-fund).

## Gas

\$DIME is intended to be the gas token for Paradex Chain (DimeVM), a general purpose smart contract platform powered by Cairo. DimeVM shares state with the Paradex Exchange, enabling a composable, high throughput execution environment where onchain applications can interact with the liquidity and privacy infrastructure built on Paradex.
