Trading fees vary based on your [Trader Profile](/docs/trading/trader-profiles). Retail traders enjoy free trading while Pro traders benefit from improved latency.

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|         | Fee    | Cap |
|---------|:------:|:---:|
| Futures | 0.015% | N/A |
