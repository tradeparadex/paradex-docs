---
title: Greeks
---

Greeks measure the sensitivity of an option's price to changes in underlying factors such as spot price, implied volatility, interest rates, and time. Paradex provides both **Raw Greeks** and **Cash Greeks**.

## Assumptions for examples

The examples below use the following assumptions:

- BTC spot price = $60,000
- Implied volatility (IV) = 40%
- Interest rate = 10%
- Position size = 1 BTC

## Spot Greeks

### Delta

Delta measures the sensitivity of an option's value to a move in the underlying spot price.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Delta | Sensitivity of option value to a move in the underlying |
| Cash Delta | Expected PnL for a 1% move in spot |

**Example:** Raw Delta = 0.5. A 1% move in spot = $600. Cash Delta = 0.5 x $600 = $300. The option gains approximately $300 if BTC rises 1%.

### Gamma

Gamma measures the sensitivity of Raw Delta to a move in the underlying spot price.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Gamma | Sensitivity of Raw Delta to a move in the underlying |
| Cash Gamma | Second-order PnL for a 1% move in spot |

**Example:** Raw Gamma = 0.0002. Cash Gamma = 1/2 x 0.0002 x $600^2 = $36. An additional ~$36 of convex PnL from a 1% BTC rise.

## Volatility Greeks

### Vega

Vega measures the sensitivity of an option's value to a 1% absolute change in implied volatility.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Vega | Sensitivity of option value to a 1% absolute change in IV |
| Cash Vega | Same as Raw Vega |

**Example:** Vega = 80. If IV moves from 40% to 41%, the option gains approximately $80.

### Vanna

Vanna measures the sensitivity of Raw Delta to a 1% absolute change in IV. Equivalently, it measures the sensitivity of Vega to a move in spot.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Vanna | Sensitivity of Raw Delta to a 1% absolute change in IV |
| Cash Vanna | Additional expected PnL assuming a 1% move in spot and a 1% increase in IV |

**Example:** Vanna = 0.01. If IV moves from 40% to 41%, Delta shifts such that the position gains an additional ~$6 per subsequent 1% spot move.

### Volga

Volga measures the sensitivity of Vega to a 1% absolute change in implied volatility.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Volga | Sensitivity of Vega to a 1% absolute change in IV |
| Cash Volga | Same as Raw Volga |

**Example:** Volga = 1.5. If IV moves from 40% to 41%, the option gains an additional ~$1.50 of convex PnL on top of Vega.

## Interest rate Greeks

### Rho

Rho measures the sensitivity of an option's value to a 1% absolute change in interest rates.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Rho | Sensitivity of option value to a 1% absolute change in interest rates |
| Cash Rho | Same as Raw Rho |

**Example:** Rho = 25. If rates move from 10% to 11%, the option gains approximately $25.

## Time Greeks

### Theta

Theta measures the sensitivity of an option's value to the passage of one day.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Raw Theta | Sensitivity of option value to 1 day of time decay |
| Cash Theta | Same as Raw Theta |

**Example:** Theta = -10. The option loses approximately $10 after 1 day, all else equal.

## Raw vs. Cash Greeks

Raw Greeks are available on the API via the [markets summary endpoint](/api/reference/markets/markets-summary). On the UI, users can choose to display Cash Greeks by changing their Greeks preference setting from **Raw** to **Cash**.
