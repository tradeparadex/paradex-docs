---
title: Real World Assets (RWA)
subtitle: Oracle pricing methodology for real world asset markets on Paradex
---

The Spot Oracle Price for RWA assets is calculated as a weighted median of prices from different sources. The weight assigned to each source is proportional to its score relative to the total score of all available feeds.

Paradex currently supports precious metals assets. More RWA asset classes will be introduced soon.

## Market hours

Precious metals markets are open 23/5, from Sunday 6:00 PM ET to Friday 5:00 PM ET, with a daily maintenance break from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM ET.

## Source scores

Source scores determine their contribution to the weighted median.

| Source | Score | Availability |
|--------|:-----:|:------------:|
| Pyth | 3 | Open hours |
| Stork | 2 | Open hours |
| Binance | 1 | Open hours |
| Hyperliquid | 1 | 24/7 |
| OKX | 1 | 24/7 |
| Lighter | 0.7 | 24/7 |
| Closing Price | 0.5 | Closing hours |
| Internal Price | 0.5 | Closing hours |

Where:

- **Closing Price** is equal to the oracle price at the market closing time.
- **Internal Price** is the EMA of the Mark Price with a 1-hour half-life. At the start of the closing period, the Internal Price is initialized to the Closing Price.

## Weights during open hours

During open hours, only sources available during open hours contribute to the weighted median.

| Source | Score | Weight |
|--------|:-----:|:------:|
| Pyth | 3 | 34.5% |
| Stork | 2 | 23.0% |
| Binance | 1 | 11.5% |
| Hyperliquid | 1 | 11.5% |
| OKX | 1 | 11.5% |
| Lighter | 0.7 | 8.0% |
| **Total** | **8.7** | **100%** |

## Weights during closing hours

During closing hours, only 24/7 sources and closing-specific sources contribute.

| Source | Score | Weight |
|--------|:-----:|:------:|
| Hyperliquid | 1 | 27.03% |
| OKX | 1 | 27.03% |
| Lighter | 0.7 | 18.92% |
| Closing Price | 0.5 | 13.51% |
| Internal Price | 0.5 | 13.51% |
| **Total** | **3.7** | **100%** |
