## v1.68.0

<ChangelogTags tags={["UI", "API"]} />

* **UI:** Add ability to attach Take Profit/ Stop Loss to a Market or Limit order in Order Builder component
* **UI:** Vaults
  * Max vault name length is now 15 symbols
  * Trade History is now available in the vault page.
* **UI:** Add funding 8hr to Mobile trade summary page.
* **UI:** In Markets Menu the `-PERP` is now omitted from market name.
* **API:** Support TPSL with parent order.
* **API:** Vaults
  * In GET /v1/vaults/summaries endpoint `tvl` was renamed to `net_deposits` and continues to indicate the net deposits in USDC.
  * Max vault name length is now 15 symbols.
