## Fees at a glance

- **Maker fees are 0%** for Retail and Pro across all Spot & Perps markets.
- **Taker fees** depend on your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles) and 14-day (14D) trading volume across your main account and all subaccounts.
- **Fees are charged on notional** (Size × Trade Price).
- **Discounts stack.** Staking, \$DIME fee discount, and FastFills (Pro only) are additive percentages applied to your volume-based taker fee.
- **Pro fees have a 1.75 bps (0.0175%) minimum**, regardless of combined discounts.

## Trading volume tiers (Retail) — Spot & Perps

Retail taker fees are determined by the user's 14-day (14D) trading volume across main account and all subaccounts.

Holders of a [MoneyBadgers](https://opensea.io/collection/moneybadgers) or [ShizoPunks](https://opensea.io/collection/shizopunks) NFT on their connected wallet qualify for reduced volume thresholds.

| Tier | 14D Volume (USD) | OR | 14D Volume (USD) + NFT | Taker Fee | Maker Fee |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Regular (VIP 0) | — | OR | — | 0.024% | 0% |
| VIP 1 | ≥ 100K | OR | NFT | 0.02% | 0% |
| VIP 2 | ≥ 500K | OR | ≥ 250K + NFT | 0.018% | 0% |
| VIP 3 | ≥ 1M | OR | ≥ 500K + NFT | 0.016% | 0% |
| VIP 4 | ≥ 5M | OR | ≥ 2.5M + NFT | 0.014% | 0% |
| VIP 5 | ≥ 10M | OR | ≥ 6M + NFT | 0.012% | 0% |
| VIP 6 | ≥ 50M | OR | ≥ 35M + NFT | 0.01% | 0% |

<sub>Fees are calculated on trade notional (Size × Trade Price).</sub>

| Tier | 14D Volume (USD) | Taker Fee | Maker Fee |
|---|---|---|---|

| Pro 2 | ≥ 5M | 0.039% | 0% |
| Pro 3 | ≥ 10M | 0.037% | 0% |
| Pro 4 | ≥ 25M | 0.035% | 0% |

<sub>Fees are calculated on trade notional (Size × Trade Price).</sub>

### Minimum fee

Pro fees are subject to a minimum of 1.75 bps (0.0175%), regardless of the total discount applied.

### FastFills discount

Pro taker fees are reduced by an additional 30% (stackable with staking and \$DIME fee discounts) when matching against retail orders. See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

## Staking discounts (Pro & Retail)

Users who stake \$DIME receive a discount on their Spot & Perps taker fees, scaling up to 30%. See [How does staking work?](#faq).

- Applies to the user's volume-based taker fees
- Stackable with \$DIME fee discount

| Staking Discount Tier | Staking Amount (\$DIME) | Fee Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Wood | 10K | 5% |
| Bronze | 50K | 10% |
| Silver | 100K | 15% |
| Gold | 500K | 20% |
| Platinum | 1M | 25% |
| Diamond | 2M | 30% |

## \$DIME fee discounts (Pro & Retail)

Users who pay their Spot & Perps trading fees in \$DIME receive an additional flat discount, deducted from their spot \$DIME deposit on Paradex.

- Flat 40% discount initially
- Stackable on top of staking discount
- Mark price exchange rate (\$DIME ↔ USDC) at the time of the user's transaction applies

<Tip title="Example" icon="fa-solid fa-lightbulb">
If a user stakes 100K \$DIME (15% staking discount) and pays trading fees in \$DIME (40% \$DIME fee discount), the total discount is 55% (15% + 40%), applied to the volume-based taker fee.
</Tip>

## Options fees (Pro & Retail)

Fees are capped at 12.5% of the option price (premium). FastFills discount applies to the fee amount.

| Profile | Maker Fee | Taker Fee |
|---|---|---|
| <Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0075% |

### FastFills discount

Options Pro taker fees are reduced by an additional 30% when matching against retail orders. See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

## Settlement fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee applies to any positions still open.

| Market | Fee |
|---|---|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |

To unstake, submit an unstaking request at any time. A 24-hour cooldown follows, after which you must confirm to receive your funds.
