title: FAQs
---

<Accordion title="Can anyone create a VTF?">
  Yes! Anyone can create a VTF as long as you deposit the minimum initial
  owner deposit of $100.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the difference between a VTF owner and a VTF operator?">
  A VTF owner is the account that controls the VTF and receives a share of
  the profits. A VTF operator is a specialized account that has access to the
  deposited funds and is responsible for trading to generate returns.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How are profits distributed in a VTF?">
  Profits are distributed based on the VTF's profit share structure. A
  percentage goes to the VTF owner, while the rest is distributed among
  depositors proportional to their share in the fund.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long after my deposit can I withdraw my funds?">
  Because withdrawal transactions cannot be performed in the same block as a
  deposit, there is currently around a **5 minute** window where you will not be
  able to withdraw funds immediately after deposit. This is the time that is
  required to create this new block for the withdrawal transaction. Otherwise,
  the time is also limited by the [Lockup
  Period](/vtfs/tutorials/withdrawal#note-you-will-not-be-able-to-withdraw-from-the-vault-if-your-minimum-lockup-period-of-not-over-yet)
  that is configured for the VTF. This can be found just above the Deposit
  button with the other VTF status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is the performance of a VTF calculated?">
  VTF performance is calculated based on the change in the fund's total value
  over time. This includes trading profits, losses, and any fees incurred.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any fees associated with using VTFs?">
  Fees may vary depending on the specific VTF. Typically, there's a profit
  share for the VTF owner. Always check the VTF's details for specific fee
  information.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How secure are Paradex VTFs?">
  Paradex VTFs are built with robust security measures, including role based
  access control and smart contract governance. However, as with any DeFi
  product, there are inherent risks, and users should do their own research
  before participating.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I monitor my VTF's performance?">
  Paradex provides detailed analytics for VTF operations. You can see an
  overview from your Portfolio and also get a more detailed analysis by clicking
  on the VTF from the [main VTFs page](https://app.paradex.trade/vaults).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will I gain any XP from depositing funds into VTFs?">
  Yes, XP will be awarded to your account on a weekly basis. VTFs earn Fee,
  Maker, OI, and TVL XP just like any normal accounts. These XP earned will then
  be distributed to depositors every Friday as VTF XP. Deposit in VTFs with
  an active XP boost for a higher earn rate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can my VTF's PnL be positive but its ROI is negative? (or vice versa)">
The ROI is calculated based on the VTF's current token price divided by the initial token price (which is $1).

The P&L and ROI of the VTF could be different due to differences in a VTF's
TVL at different time periods.

A VTF's P&L could be positive due to higher returns on a higher TVL base
(somebody had deposited money in the VTF), but negative in ROI because it had
negative returns on a smaller TVL base.

</Accordion>
