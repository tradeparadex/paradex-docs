<Accordion title="v1.123.8 — Wallet Support:" defaultOpen={true}>

  <Badge color="green">PROD  Dec 5th</Badge> <Badge color="yellow">TESTNET  Dec 4th</Badge>

  **🖥️ UI Updates**
  - Wallet Support:
  -  We now support `Keplr` and `Xverse` Starknet wallets! You can connect using your preferred wallet.
  - The bars in the `orderbook` are now larger and fuller, making the data easier to read at a glance.
  - New `orderbook` animation to make real-time updates clearer. You can easily toggle this feature ON or OFF in your settings.
  - Volume Bars: The y-axis for volume bars now auto-scales, providing a clearer view of recent trading activity.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="v1.123.7 — Added 100ms as a new interactive update frequency option for" defaultOpen={false}>

  <Badge color="green">PROD  Dec 4th</Badge> <Badge color="yellow">TESTNET  Dec 3rd</Badge>

  **⚙️ Engine Updates**
  - Added `100ms` as a new interactive update frequency option for `orderbook` subscriptions (in addition to 200ms and 400ms).

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="v1.123.5 — Market Menu search input is cleared upon selecting a market" defaultOpen={false}>

  <Badge color="green">PROD  Dec 3rd</Badge> <Badge color="yellow">TESTNET  Dec 3rd</Badge>

  **🖥️ UI Updates**
  - Market Menu search input is cleared upon selecting a market.

  **⚙️ Engine Updates**
  - Use `average fill price` for max slippage calculation for `MARKET` order ([detailed docs](/docs/trading/placing-orders/vwap-price-protection)).
  - `max_slippage_price` field is deprecated and replaced by `vwap_price` now.

</Accordion>
