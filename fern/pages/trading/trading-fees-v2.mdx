## Your base fees

Maker fees are 0% for all tiers and profiles. Your taker fee depends on your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles) and 14-day trading volume across main account and all subaccounts.

## Lower your fees

Stack these discounts to reduce your Spot & Perps taker fee. Pro fees have a minimum of 1.75 bps (0.0175%) regardless of total discount.

### Stake \$DIME, up to 30% off

Stake \$DIME to receive a discount on your taker fees. See [How does staking work?](#faq).

| Staking Tier | Staking Amount (\$DIME) | Fee Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Wood | 10K | 5% |
| Bronze | 50K | 10% |
| Silver | 100K | 15% |
| Gold | 500K | 20% |
| Platinum | 1M | 25% |
| Diamond | 2M | 30% |

### Pay fees in \$DIME, 40% off

Pay your trading fees in \$DIME for a flat 40% discount, deducted from your spot \$DIME deposit on Paradex. Stackable on top of staking discount.

- Mark price exchange rate (\$DIME ↔ USDC) at the time of the transaction applies

<Tip title="Example" icon="fa-solid fa-lightbulb">
100K \$DIME staked (15% staking discount) + \$DIME fee payment (40%) = 55% total discount on your taker fee.
</Tip>

### FastFills, 30% off (Pro only)

Pro taker fees are reduced by 30% when matching against retail orders. Stackable with staking and \$DIME fee discounts. See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.

## Options fees

Fees are capped at 12.5% of the option price (premium). FastFills discount applies to the fee amount.

| Profile | Maker Fee | Taker Fee |
|---|---|---|
| <Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0075% |
| <Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge> | 0.0075% | 0.0125% |

Options Pro taker fees are reduced by 30% via [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) when matching against retail orders.

## Settlement fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee applies to any positions still open.

| Market | Fee |
|---|---|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How does staking work?">
Stake \$DIME to unlock tiered fee discounts ranging from 5% (Wood) to 30% (Diamond), applied to your volume-based taker fees and stackable with the \$DIME fee discount.

To unstake, submit an unstaking request at any time. A 24-hour cooldown follows, after which you must confirm to receive your funds.

For more on \$DIME, see [\$DIME Utility](/docs/getting-started/dime-utility).
</Accordion>
