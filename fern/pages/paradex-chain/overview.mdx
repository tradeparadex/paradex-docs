Paradex Chain is a dedicated application chain built on the Starknet Stack, combining the performance of a purpose-built blockchain with full compatibility with the Starknet ecosystem. Developers can deploy smart contracts, access Paradex's deep liquidity infrastructure, and build on a chain designed for privacy and speed.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Starknet Stack" icon="fa-solid fa-cubes" href="/chain/paradex-chain-introduction">
    Built on the CairoVM with full Starknet compatibility. Deploy existing Starknet contracts by simply updating RPC endpoints.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Deep Liquidity Access" icon="fa-solid fa-water" href="/chain/paradex-chain-introduction">
    Seamlessly interact with the deep liquidity on the Paradex Exchange directly from the chain.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Privacy by Default" icon="fa-solid fa-lock" href="/chain/security/security-lockdown">
    Encrypted state diffs with ZK verification posted to Ethereum. Custom RPC masking protects all position and account data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
