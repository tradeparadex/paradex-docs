|            | <Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge> | <Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge> |

| Speed Bump | Order Submission: 500ms <br /> Order Cancellation: 300ms | None |

## Settlement Fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|         | Fee    | Cap |
|---------|:------:|:---:|
| Futures | 0.015% | N/A |
| Options | 0.010% | 25% |

<sub>Fees are calculated based on notional value.</sub>
