# Marketplace

The Paradex Agent Marketplace lets you publish your trading signal or bot so other users can subscribe and trade alongside it.

## Publishing Your Signal / Bot

To publish to the marketplace:

1. Build your strategy using the [MCP server](/agentic-ai-hub/mcp) or [CLI](/agentic-ai-hub/cli)
2. Verify consistent performance on testnet
3. Submit your integration for review via [Discord](https://discord.gg/paradex)

Once approved, your strategy will be listed in the marketplace where users can subscribe and allocate capital to it.

## Requirements

- Strategy must be built on top of Paradex MCP or CLI
- Must include a description of the strategy logic and risk profile
- Minimum track record on testnet before mainnet listing
- Must comply with Paradex terms of service
