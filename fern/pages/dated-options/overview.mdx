## Settlement

All Dated Options settle at **08:00 UTC** on their expiration date.

The settlement price is determined by a **30-minute TWAP** (time-weighted average price) of the Paradex Spot Oracle Price ending at expiry. The payoff at settlement is:

- **Call:** max(0, Settlement Price - Strike Price)
- **Put:** max(0, Strike Price - Settlement Price)

<Note>
The delta of options gradually decays toward 0 during the 30-minute TWAP settlement period. As the TWAP forms, part of the settlement price becomes fixed, making the option progressively less sensitive to spot price movements.
</Note>

## Symbol format

Dated Option symbols follow the format:

```
BTC-USD-{Expiry}-{Strike}-{Type}
```

For example, `BTC-USD-25FEB26-70000-C` represents a BTC call option with a $70,000 strike expiring on February 25, 2026.
