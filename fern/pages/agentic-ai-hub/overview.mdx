Paradex provides a suite of AI-native tools for onchain trading, digital asset management, and data analytics. These tools are designed to serve as a first-class interface between AI agents and live crypto markets — including spot, perpetual futures, options, and real-world assets (RWAs) such as commodities, tokenized stocks, and forex.

AI agents have specific infrastructure requirements that most trading platforms don't meet. Paradex addresses all three:
