## v1.67.0

<ChangelogTags tags={["UI", "API"]} />

* **UI:** Max vault name length is now 35 symbols
* **UI:** Added `Avg.Fill Price` to open orders and order history table.
* **UI:** Trade page, order book update frequency updated from 50ms to 100ms.
* **UI:** Fixed the `remember me` tooltip overlapping `continue` in `link wallet` modal.
* **UI:** Revamped balances page within portfolio.
* **UI:** Fix estimated liquidation prices for limit and stop limit orders - use limit price as base.
* **UI:** Vaults
  * You can now see the unwinding progress of your vault withdrawals in the relevant vault page or in the portfolio page, under the vaults tab.
  * The `Lockup Remaining` was moved from the top of the vault page to the `My vault deposit` section
  * You can now see the vaults you invested in on the portfolio page, under the `Vaults` tab, including the `Lockup Remaining`
* **API:** Introduced `delta` to perpetual future's GET `/markets/summary` endpoint.
