Portfolio Margin is currently in beta and only available to whitelisted users.
</Note>

Portfolio Margin evaluates risk at the portfolio level rather than per-position. The **Initial Margin Requirement (IMR)** is:

$$
\text{IMR} = \text{Net Portfolio Initial Margin Requirement} + \text{Fee Provision}
$$

where the Net Portfolio Initial Margin Requirement is the greater of **SCAN Risk** and **Minimum Delta Requirement**.

**Fee Provision** is the expected funding payment over the next 8 hours, reserving margin to cover upcoming funding obligations.

The **Maintenance Margin Requirement (MMR)** is:

$$
\text{MMR} = 50\% \times \text{Net Portfolio Initial Margin Requirement} + \text{Fee Provision}
$$

## SCAN risk

SCAN Risk stress-tests the portfolio across **24 scenarios** by shocking spot price and implied volatility simultaneously. The SCAN Risk equals the maximum weighted loss across all scenarios.

Each scenario applies:

$$
\text{Shocked Spot} = \text{Spot Price} \times (1 + \text{Spot Shock})
$$

$$
\text{Shocked IV} = \text{Mark IV} \times \bigg(1 + \text{Vol Shock}
\times \Big(\frac{30}{\max(\text{DTE Floor}, \text{DTE})}\Big)^{\text{Vega Power}}\bigg)
$$

where:
- **DTE Floor** = 1
- **Vega Power** = 0.30 (30%) if DTE < 30, or 0.13 (13%) if DTE ≥ 30
- **Min Vol Shock Up** = 40% — floor applied to the upside vol shock

These values are common across all assets.

## Minimum delta requirement

The Minimum Delta Requirement charges based on the portfolio's net and hedged delta exposure:

$$
\text{Min Delta Risk} = (\text{Unhedged Margin Factor} \times |\text{Net Delta}| + \text{Hedged Margin Factor} \times \text{Hedged Delta}) \times \text{Spot Price}
$$

where:

$$
\text{Hedged Delta} = \frac{\text{Gross Delta} - |\text{Net Delta}|}{2}
$$

### Min-delta coefficients

| Asset | Unhedged Margin Factor | Hedged Margin Factor |
|-------|:----------------------:|:--------------------:|
| BTC   | 2%                     | 1%                   |
| ETH   | 2%                     | 1%                   |
| HYPE  | 3%                     | 1.5%                 |

<Accordion title="Example calculation">

**Portfolio:**
- Long 1 BTC-USD-PERP (delta = +1)
- Short 5 BTC 70k Calls (delta = +0.3 each)

**Calculations:**
- Net Delta = +1 - 5(0.3) = -0.5
- Gross Delta = 1 + 5(0.3) = 2.5
- Hedged Delta = (2.5 - 0.5) / 2 = 1.0

With BTC Spot = $70,000:

$$
\text{Min Delta Risk} = (2\% \times 0.5 + 1\% \times 1.0) \times 70{,}000 = \$1{,}400
$$

</Accordion>
