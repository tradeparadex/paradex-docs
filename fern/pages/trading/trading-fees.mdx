---
title: Trading Fees
subtitle: Fee structure for trading on Paradex
---

Trading fees vary based on your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles).

<Tip title="FastFills taker discount" icon="fa-solid fa-bolt">
Pro taker fees are reduced by 30% when matching against retail orders.<br/>
See [FastFills](/trading/fastfills) for details.
</Tip>

## Spot & futures fees

| Profile | Maker | Taker |
|---------|:---:|:---:|
| <Tooltip tip="UI Default"><Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge></Tooltip> | 0% | 0.0075% |
| <Tooltip tip="API Default"><Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge></Tooltip> | 0% | <Tooltip tip="Eligible for FastFills taker discount">0.02%</Tooltip> |

<sub>Fees are calculated on trade notional (Size * Trade Price)</sub>

## Dated options fees

| Profile | Maker | Taker |
|---------|:---:|:---:|
| <Tooltip tip="UI Default"><Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge></Tooltip> | 0.0075% | 0.0075% |
| <Tooltip tip="API Default"><Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge></Tooltip> | 0.0075% | <Tooltip tip="Eligible for FastFills taker discount">0.0075%</Tooltip> |

<sub>1. Dated option fees are calculated on underlying spot notional (Size × Spot Price)</sub><br/>
<sub>2. Fees are always capped at 12.5% of Option Premium (Size × Option Trade Price)</sub><br />
<sub>3. FastFills taker discount only applies to the base fee rate and does not affect the 12.5% cap</sub>

## Settlement fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|                          | Fee    |
|--------------------------|:------:|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |
