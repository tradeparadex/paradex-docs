Paradex provides a suite of AI-native tools for onchain trading, digital asset management, and data analytics. These tools are designed to serve as a first-class interface between AI agents and live crypto markets — including spot, perpetual futures, options, and TradFi markets such as commodities, tokenized stocks, and forex.

## Why Paradex for AI Agents?

AI agents have specific infrastructure requirements that most trading platforms don't meet. Paradex addresses all three:

| Requirement | Paradex Solution |
|---|---|
| Low transaction costs | Competitive [fee structure](/trading/trading-fees) |
| Privacy | Full onchain privacy |
| Speed & security | ZK-proof backed execution |
