---
title: Deposit
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a VTF">
    Select a VTF on the VTFs page that you wish to deposit in.

    <img src="../../../assets/vaults/vault_deposit_select.png" alt=""/>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Deposit">
    On the VTF Stats page, click on Deposit at the top right hand corner.

    <img src="../../../assets/vaults/vault_deposit_button.png" alt=""/>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Enter the Amount you wish to deposit in the VTF.

    <img src="../../../assets/vaults/vault_deposit_amount.png" alt=""/>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Deposit">
    Find your amount deposited at the "My VTF Deposit" section.

    <img src="../../../assets/vaults/vault_deposit_confirmation.png" alt=""/>
  </Step>
</Steps>
