## v1.123.8

<ChangelogTags tags={["UI"]} />

* Wallet Support:
  -  We now support `Keplr` and `Xverse` Starknet wallets! You can connect using your preferred wallet.
* The bars in the `orderbook` are now larger and fuller, making the data easier to read at a glance.
* New `orderbook` animation to make real-time updates clearer. You can easily toggle this feature ON or OFF in your settings.
* Volume Bars: The y-axis for volume bars now auto-scales, providing a clearer view of recent trading activity.
