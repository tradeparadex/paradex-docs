---
title: Render
subtitle: Similar to Railway with a free tier. Free services spin down after 15 minutes of inactivity and have a cold start (~30 s) on the next request.
---

<Steps>
### Create a web service

Go to [render.com](https://render.com) → **New → Web Service**.

### Connect your repository

Connect your GitHub repo. Render detects the `Dockerfile`.

### Set environment variables

Set the following under **Environment**:

| Key | Value |
|---|---|
| `MCP_TRANSPORT` | `streamable-http` |
| `MCP_PORT` | `8080` |
| `PARADEX_ENVIRONMENT` | `prod` |

### Configure the health check

Set **Health Check Path** to `/health`.

### Deploy

Click **Create Web Service**. Your endpoint is `https://<service-name>.onrender.com/mcp`.

### Connect your AI client

Paste the URL into your AI client's MCP settings. See the [Remote MCP quickstart](/agentic-ai-hub/remote-mcp/quickstart#connect-your-ai-client) for client-specific instructions.

</Steps>

---

Free tier sleeps after 15 min of inactivity — upgrade to Starter (~$7/mo) to avoid cold starts. Render provides automatic TLS and a stable URL.
