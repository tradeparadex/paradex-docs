---
title: Trading Fees
subtitle: Fee structure for trading on Paradex
---

Trading fees vary based on your [Trader Profile](/trading/trader-profiles). All fees are calculated based on notional value.

<Info>
Dated option fees are capped at 12.5% of the option premium.
</Info>

## Orderbook fees

| Profile | Maker | Taker |
|---------|:---:|:---:|
| <Tooltip tip="UI Default"><Badge intent="success" outlined minimal>Retail</Badge></Tooltip> | 0% | 0.0075% |
| <Tooltip tip="API Default"><Badge intent="note" outlined minimal>Pro</Badge></Tooltip> | 0% | 0.02% |

<Tip>
**FastFill taker discount** — Pro taker fee is reduced by 30% to **0.014%** when matching against retail orders. See [FastFill](/trading/fastfill) for details.
</Tip>

## Settlement fees

When a market is delisted, a settlement fee is applied to close any positions still open in that market.

|                          | Fee    |
|--------------------------|:------:|
| Spot and Perpetual Futures | 0.015% |
| Dated Options | 0.0075% |
