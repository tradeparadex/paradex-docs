## Release Sequence

During each release, the platform transitions through three modes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="CANCEL_ONLY (~2 min)">
    Clean state preparation before deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="POST_ONLY (~2 min)">
    Markets stabilize with maker-only liquidity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OK">
    Full trading resumes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Price Protection

Orders in CANCEL_ONLY and POST_ONLY modes must be within **±5 bps** of the External Fair Price. Orders outside this band are automatically canceled.

### Example

BTC with an External Fair Price of $100,000:

| | Band Limit | <span style={{color: '#22c55e'}}>Accepted</span> | <span style={{color: '#ef4444'}}>Auto-Canceled</span> |
|---|:----------:|:--------:|:-------------:|
| Buy | $100,050 | Price ≤ $100,050 | Price > $100,050 |
| Sell | $99,950 | Price ≥ $99,950 | Price < $99,950 |

### How the External Fair Price Is Calculated

$
\text{External Fair Price} = \text{Spot Oracle Price} \times (1 + \text{External Basis Rate})
$

$
\text{External Basis Rate} = \operatorname{median}(\text{Binance Basis},\ \text{OKX Basis},\ \text{Bybit Basis},\ \text{Hyperliquid Basis},\ \text{Paradex Basis EMA})
$
