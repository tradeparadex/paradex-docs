## Android (Chrome)

* Step 1: Head over to [https://app.paradex.trade/](https://app.paradex.trade/trade/BTC-USD-PERP)

* Step 3: Select "Add to Home Screen" <img src="../../../assets/photo_2024-07-16 14.56.20 copy.jpeg" alt="" data-size="line"/>
