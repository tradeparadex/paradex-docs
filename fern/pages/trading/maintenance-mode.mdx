## Release Sequence

During each release, the platform transitions through three modes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="CANCEL_ONLY (~2 min)">
    Clean state preparation before deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="POST_ONLY (~2 min)">
    Markets stabilize with maker-only liquidity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OK">
    Full trading resumes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Price Protection

During CANCEL_ONLY and POST_ONLY modes, a price band of ±5 basis points around the External Fair Price protects against unfair pricing.

<Warning>
Orders outside the band are automatically canceled — both resting and incoming.
</Warning>

### Example

BTC with an External Fair Price of $100,000:

| | Band Limit | <Icon icon="fa-solid fa-circle-check" color="#22c55e" /> Accepted | <Icon icon="fa-solid fa-circle-xmark" color="#ef4444" /> Auto-Canceled |
|---|:----------:|:--------:|:-------------:|
| Buy | $100,050 | Price ≤ $100,050 | Price > $100,050 |
| Sell | $99,950 | Price ≥ $99,950 | Price < $99,950 |

### How the External Fair Price Is Calculated

$$
\text{External Fair Price} = \text{Spot Oracle Price} \times (1 + \text{External Basis Rate})
$$

External Basis Rate = median of basis rates from Binance, OKX, Bybit, Hyperliquid, and Paradex (1h EMA).
